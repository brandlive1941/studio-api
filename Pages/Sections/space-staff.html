Space Staff

This step allows you to add your sponsor space staffers' names, e-mail addresses, login IDs, and passwords. When the staffers log into the event, they are displayed in your sponsor space under the Space Staff tab and can interact with sponsor space visitors. It is important that you determine your sponsor space staff and ensure their availability for the sneak preview day and the live presentation day. Like an in-person presentation, having the right staff supporting your sponsor space can make all the difference in the quality of the overall experience for the presentation attendees.

Space Staff allows you to add your sponsor space representatives and register them for the event by configuring their login and password information.

Note: The information you provide in this step will be used as the event login information. However, each sponsor space staff member will be able to set up and modify their own profiles, including login and contact information, once they log into the event.

To add a staff member that already has a profile (typically only the sponsor space administrator):

  1. Click Add New Staffer.
  2. Select the user’s name from the Select User drop-down menu.
  3. Click Save Changes.

To add a new staff member for your sponsor space:

  1. Click Add New Staffer.
  2. Click Create New User.
  3. Fill out the following fields:

In the Personal Info tab (required):

  • Name: Enter the full name of your new sponsor space staff member.
  • Type: If applicable, select the type of user from the drop-down menu. Typical options include Staff and Administrator. Access to administrative features may be limited based upon the user type.
  • Email Address: Provide a valid email address (unique to each member).
  • Login ID: Provide the login name the user will use to access the event.
  • Password: Create a password for the user.
  • Time Zone: Select the time zone that the user works in. All times displayed n the event are adjusted to reflect the user’s local time.

In the Contact Info tab (optional):

  • All tabs are optional and may be filled out by the user when they log into the event. You may fill out the contact information if desired.
  1. Click Save Changes.
  2. The new staff member will appear in the main Space Staff window.

To modify an existing staff member’s profile:

  1. The main Space Staff window displays a list of all of the staff members assigned to the sponsor space.
  2. Click on the Edit link on the same row as the staffer’s name.
  3. Modify the user’s profile as needed. You may upload the user’s picture if desired.
  4. Click Save Changes.

Once you have added your sponsor space staff members and configured their information, click Apply Changes.

Note: You can go back and edit any step at any time during the sponsor space building process.
