Space Colors

These can be your company’s colors or colors that complement or highlight your company logo.

 

This step allows you to choose two (2) colors for your sponsor space. One is your primary (or main) space color, the other is your secondary (or accent) color.

To choose your space colors, you can do one of the following:

  1. Select your colors from the drop-down menu of the most popular color choices.
  2. Click the Define Custom Color button and pick a color from the Color Selection grid (see image below). Once you have chosen the color that you want to use, click Save in the lower right-hand corner of the Color Selection window to save your selection.
  3. Enter a specific shade of color by entering the six-digit HEX value (i.e. # CCCCCC).

Once you have made your selection, it will display in the main work area.

Once you have selected your space colors, click Apply Changes.
