Format and Requirements

Refer to the following technical requirements when uploading graphics and other content to your sponsor space. By adhering to these guidelines, you will ensure that all graphics in your sponsor space will be displayed at the highest quality possible, and all of the documents in your sponsor space will be downloaded quickly and easily by visitors. This will produce the most aesthetically pleasing and technically efficient experience for each visitor.

Note: Not all features may be available in your event. Please check with your event host or your primary event contact to find out which features are applicable to your event.
